Custom Rides

Intamin Wing Coaster

Description: Riders sit four abreast in wide trains, as they speed through large drops and airtime hills

Notes: Has sprites for vertical slopes, but the track style does not provide verticals, so they aren't used.

Maurer Sohne Spinning Coaster

Description: Riders sit back-to-back in individual cars, as they twist and spin through tight drops and turns

Notes: Uses fake spinning.

Screenshots

Raft Ride

Description: Riders sit in large inflatable rafts, gently spinning as they glide down a twisting semi-circular track.

Notes: Uses fake spinning. There are some graphical glitches on wide turns as the track is a bit small for the rafts. This ride will produce splashes if merged onto water coaster track.

Screenshots

Body slide

Description: Riders slide on their backs down steep drops and fast turns

Notes: The slide must be a full circuit, even though real body slides aren't. Therefore the lift should probably be hidden or hacked out. Sprites for diagonals and verticals are provided, but not used.

Tube Slide (Single Tubes)

Description: Guests slide down a twisting track in inflatable rubber rings

Notes: Same as the body slide, but the lift doesn't look so bad on this one because it can act as a tube return.

Tube Slide (Double Tubes)

Description: Guests slide down a twisting track in inflatable rubber rings

Notes: This ride has tubes that take two people; it is otherwise identical to the single tubes version.

Tube Slide (Single Tubes) - No empty tubes

Description: Guests slide down a twisting track in inflatable rubber rings

Notes: This version of the tube slide has the tubes as part of the peep sprites, so they don't show when empty. This is intended for those who want to hack out the lift. A consequence of this is that you can't change the color of the tubes - they're yellow.

Tube Slide (Double Tubes) - No empty tubes

Description: Guests slide down a twisting track in inflatable rubber rings

Notes: Same as above, but with the tubes that take two riders.
